Account Validation Rules Import

Overview

The Approval chains Import process reads files from ./Incoming/AccountValidationRules/ in the SFTP. These files will be moved to the archive folder located at ./Archive/Incoming/AccountValidationRules/ before being processed in Alpha-Numeric order.
