# Build on the Coupa Platform

Our API-based open integration platform lets technology partners and independent software
vendors (ISVs) integrate their products with Coupa

As part of Coupa’s Platform Vision, we are creating new
ways for customers and partners to build on top of Coupa with
embedded Apps. Coupa customers use products beyond Coupa to solve
their business spend management needs. With the right platform
partnerships and enhancements, we can create a better end-user
experience, bringing more value to our customers.
