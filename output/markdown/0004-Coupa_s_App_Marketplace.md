# Coupa's App Marketplace

Learn about and install Coupa Partner applications within your Coupa instance.

You can access the App Marketplace at [https://marketplace.coupa.com](https://marketplace.coupa.com).
There, you can browse our selection of apps by category, or check
out some of our featured apps, newest apps, and most popular
enterprise-ready apps.

![](https://compass.coupa.com/_dita_/en-us/documentation/plat/integ/coupa_platform/images/image1.png)

## Certified apps

Certified apps allow you to download and use applications with
confidence knowing which ones have been vetted in detail and
approved by Coupa. Coupa certifies embedded apps and integration
components such as OAuth clients. You can:

- View a green checkbox next to Coupa certified apps.

- View your installed and certified apps in the [Integration Control Center](https://compass.coupa.com/x330965.xml).

- Clearly see the difference between Community Exchange
applications and Marketplace applications.

- Filter tables by certified apps.

The following objects can be certified: embedded apps, API
connectors, custom API integrations, OAuth clients.

## View installed apps

You can view your installed apps in two tables:

| **Path** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| **Setup > Platform > Installed Apps and Content** | Contains all installed apps that were initiated through the Community Exchange UI, which at this time is Coupa and customer apps. |
| **Setup > Platform > Installed Marketplace Apps** | Contains all installed apps that were initiated through the App Marketplace, which at this time displays partner apps only. |
