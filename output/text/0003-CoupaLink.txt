CoupaLink

CoupaLink is Coupa's API-based open integration platform. It allows technology partners and independent software vendors (ISVs) to integrate their products with Coupa. CoupaLink supports APIs for various solution areas like:

-

Contracts,

-

Tax Engines,

-

ERP Integrations,

-

Invoicing

-

Payments

-

Travel and Expenses

-

Middleware Platforms

Partners are able to work closely with Coupa to define streamlined business processes integrated via the APIs that result in a well-architected end to end solution for our customers. These integrated solutions help our customers adopt Coupa faster at a significantly lower cost resulting in quantifiable value for their organizations.

Go to https://www.coupa.com/coupalink/ to learn more.
