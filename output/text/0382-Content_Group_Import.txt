Content Group Import

Overview

Content Groups are sometimes called Business Groups in Coupa. The Content Groups Import process read files from ./Incoming/BusinessGroups/ in the SFTP. These files will be moved to the archive folder located at ./Incoming/Archive/BusinessGroups/ before being processed in alphanumeric order.
