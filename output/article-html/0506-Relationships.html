Relationships

The Relationships quick integration import allows Coupa users to directly upload spreadsheets via the HTTPS protocol. This allows customers to perform inbound imports in an automated manner without the need of a relationship interface.

Post URL

https://<CRA_TENANT>.hiperos.com/QuickIntegration/Relationships
