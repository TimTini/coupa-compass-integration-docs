The Coupa Supplier Portal (CSP) has a token-based system for specific advanced invoicing features. This provides greater flexibility, cost control, and visibility into supplier usage of invoicing functionalities. Invoicing features with token cost Suppliers consume tokens for each use of the selected advanced features: - InvoiceSmash: for each invoice uploaded.
