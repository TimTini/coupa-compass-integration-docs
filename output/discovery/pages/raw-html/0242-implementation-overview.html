Implementation Overview

When it comes to implementing your Coupa business spend management platform, you can count on Coupa and our trusted partner ecosystem to ensure your success.

Companies around the world have implemented Coupa to manage their spend management initiatives, delivering billions in savings,managing risk, and improving operating effectiveness. Coupa implementation projects are focused on one thing: ensuring customer success. To us, that means jointly focusing on our customer’s unique spend management goals, defining the success metrics that will deliver on those goals, and ensuring stakeholder alignment across all phases of the implementation project to speed time to value.

Implementation Programs

Coupa platform is designed to scale along and drive value to our customers regardless of their size or industry. Whether you are a growing technology startup or a large established global manufacturing organization, Coupa tailors an implementation services approach that enables you to operationalize your spend management goals and sets you up to extract maximum value out of your subscription.

Direct Delivery

Coupa led Value Services model across mid to larger size customer market segment that goes through a four-step implementation methodology to deliver a designed and integrated solution tailored to customer’s specific business spend objectives and goals.  Learn more about Direct Delivery.

Expert Services

In the world of modern spend management, Coupa Expert Services serves as the ultimate catalyst for transforming your procurement, supply chain, and treasury operations into a unified engine of efficiency. Our team of certified specialists goes beyond standard technical implementation; we partner with your organization to embed industry best practices, optimize complex workflows, and ensure you are capturing the full value of the Coupa platform. By aligning our deep domain expertise with your specific business objectives, we help you eliminate spend leakage, mitigate global risk, and achieve the operational agility required to thrive in a volatile market. Learn more about all our Expert Services.

Co-Delivery

The Coupa Co-Delivery model represents a specialized, high-touch engagement framework designed specifically for our most strategic enterprise customers. Moving beyond standard support, Co-Delivery is a shared engagement where Coupa and our certified Partners operate as a unified team to ensure project success. In this model, Coupa maintains an active, hands-on role in the creation of key deliverables, providing a direct link to Coupa’s own product expertise while leveraging the specialized implementation strengths of our Partners. Each engagement is custom-scoped and sized based on your specific modules and business use cases, ensuring that your digital transformation is backed by the collective intelligence and accountability of both organizations. Learn more about Co-Delivery.
